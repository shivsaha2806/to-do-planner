🔧 Firebase Auth Setup Guide

Your planner is running in Local Storage Mode. All features work completely, but tasks are saved locally on this browser.

How to enable Cloud Sync & Google Auth in your Firebase:

  1. Go to your Firebase Console.
  2. On the left sidebar, click Build → Authentication.
  3. Under the Sign-in method tab, enable both Anonymous and Google providers, then save changes!
